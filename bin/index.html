Geometrize

Geometrizing images into geometric primitives


Test cases demonstrating the web export functionality of Geometrize, an application for recreating images with geometric primitives.

All the graphics on this page are composed of simple shapes arranged to approximate images. Graphics on the left are drawn on HTML5 canvas, those on the right are drawn with WebGL three.js.
